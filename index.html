WHAT IS FUNDVISTAX?

Fundvistax is an end-to-end digital advisory, token issuance, investment, trading* and custody* platform. We provide the full suite of services for companies to harness the full potential of blockchain technologies in the capital markets.

*Fundvistax currently provides OTC trading and is pending approval for a Recognized Market Operator license.
*Custody is provided by licensed digital asset custodian Hex Trust Pte. Ltd.

Illiquidity and Low Trading Volumes

Private market trading volume is extremely low with annual volumes around ~USD0.1 trillion which is a stark contrast to public market trading which is around ~US$35 trillion

Issuers Lack Access

Currently only accessible to well established issuers. Mid-stage issuers (venture capital, private equity, RE companies) have very limited to no access

Poor Asset Discovery

Lack of connectivity between market participants and service providers reducing ability to discover new assets

Poor Price Discovery

Fragmented infrastructure and lack of trading venues causing difficulty to collate volume and to facilitate price discovery

Lack of Trust and Transparency

There is currently a lack of transparency with private assets as by its nature are “private” and lack visibility to financial performance compared to public markets

Investors Lack Access

Currently only access to Private Banks, Institutional investors or high net worth individuals (HNWI), all other investors have limited to no access to these investments
